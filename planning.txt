Discord Coach Bot

Main features:
- add activities (`/addactivity`)
- view activities (`/activities`)
- delete activities (`/deleteactivity`)
- add equipment (`/addequipment`)
- view equipment (`/equipment`)
- retire equipment (`/retireequipment`)
- delete equipment (`/equipment`)
- weekly summaries (automatically & `/weekly [sport]`)
- monthly summaries (automatically on first day 9am & `/monthly <month> [sport]`)
- leaderboard (`/leaderboard`)
- challenges
- training plans
- cycling and triathlon race notifications and results (`/race ...`)
- Strava integration
